About me

Tao Zhang(张涛 in Chinese) is currently a second year master’s student at Nuclear Power Institute of China(NPIC), under the guidance of professor Jiao Yongjun. He completed his undergraduate studies at Tsinghua University in 2022, majoring in nuclear engineering and technology, with a minor in computer science. He currently is a visiting student of AI for Scientific Simulation and Discovery Lab at Westlake University, mentored by Tailin Wu.

His research focuses on: AI for science & nuclear, multiphysics fields simulation.
