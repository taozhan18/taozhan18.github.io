    Tao Zhang

    About me

    Zhang Tao (Chinese: 张涛) is currently a second year master’s student at Nuclear Power Institute of China(NPIC), under the guidance of professor Jiao Yongjun. He completed his undergraduate studies at Tsinghua University in 2022, majoring in nuclear engineering and technology, with a minor in computer science. He is currently a visiting student at the Artificial Intelligence Science Simulation and Discovery Laboratory of West Lake University, conducting research on the intersection of nuclear energy and AI. His supervisor is Wu Tailin. Her research focuses on: AI for nuclear, multiphysics fields simulation, and reactor design.
